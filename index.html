    How It Works

    Four AI roles working together for smarter e-waste decisions.

    1

    Classification

    Keyword matching plus Gemini's natural language understanding identifies the item category and hazard tier from a free-text description or photo.

    2

    Information Retrieval

    A structured knowledge base of e-waste categories, materials, disposal routes, and CO₂ data grounds the AI's responses with verified facts.

    3

    Summarization

    Gemini condenses complex hazard and disposal information into concise, actionable guidance tailored to the specific item described.

    4

    Conversational Guidance

    The chat assistant maintains context across turns to help users explore repair options, safety questions, local regulations, and more.

    Responsible AI

    EcoSort AI is built with fairness, transparency, and user safety as first principles.

    ⚖ Fairness

    Guidance is category-based, not user-specific. No personal data is stored or used to differentiate recommendations.

    🔍 Transparency

    Every result shows its source (local keyword match vs. Gemini) and confidence level. The AI's role is clearly explained.

    🛡 Safety

    The assistant is constrained by a strict system prompt to flag urgent hazards, refuse dangerous advice, and always escalate to professionals.

    🔒 Privacy

    No analytics, no cookies, no external trackers. Chat history lives only in your browser session memory and is never persisted server-side.

    🧭 Ethics

    The system explicitly refuses to recommend illegal dumping, burning, or unsafe dismantling — and always cites certified disposal channels.

    📢 Disclaimer

    EcoSort AI is a guidance tool, not a certified hazardous-waste authority. For high-risk or emergency cases, always contact local professionals.
